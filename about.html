About Me

Sana Arif

Hi there, I'm Sana, a 27-year-old who loves
reading and sipping coffee. I believe in living life
fully and indulging in things that feed our souls.

My favourite qoutes is by C.S. Lewis:
"You never know what you can do until you try,
and very few try unless they have to."

I enjoy reading a variety of genres, but
I have a particular fondness
for non-fiction, spiritual, philosophical,
poetry, and historical works. I am drawn to
books that inspire me
and enrich my soul, helping me continue growing.
